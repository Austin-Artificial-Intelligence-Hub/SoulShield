◈ SoulShield Demo Script

Video Duration: ~2 minutes 30 seconds

1. Opening 0:00 - 0:10
Show
SoulShield landing page / login screen
Narrator
"This is SoulShield — an AI support companion for survivors of trauma. Let me show you how it works."
2. Login & Interface 0:10 - 0:25
Do
Log in with demo account → Show main chat interface
Narrator
"After logging in, you see a simple, calming interface. Quick actions help users start a conversation when they don't know what to say."
Show
Hover over the quick action buttons briefly
3. Normal Support Demo 0:25 - 0:50
Type this
"I can't stop thinking about what happened"
Wait
Show typing indicator → Response appears with options
Narrator
"The AI responds with empathy — no diagnosis, no advice. It validates feelings and offers grounding options. Notice the clickable response buttons."
Key Point
Point out: "It never tells you what to do — it offers choices."
4. Bystander Mode ⭐ 0:50 - 1:30
Do
Click "New Session" to start fresh
Narrator
"Now watch what happens when a user indicates they're not safe to talk freely."
Type this (slowly)
"He checks my phone every night. I just need something to help me get through today."
Wait
Response appears — neutral wellness language
Narrator
"The AI automatically detects the privacy signal. Look at the response — no words like 'abuse' or 'escape.' It looks like a normal wellness app."
Highlight
"But notice: 'You have the right to take care of yourself.' 'You have options.' The survivor understands — the abuser just sees a mindfulness app."
5. Voice Features 1:30 - 1:50
Do
Click the microphone button → Show "Listening..." state
Narrator
"Voice input — for users who can't safely type."
Do
Click "Voice replies: Off" toggle → Shows "Voice replies: On"
Narrator
"Voice replies — so they can listen instead of reading."
6. Dark Mode 1:50 - 2:00
Do
Click the sun/moon icon in header → Interface switches to dark
Narrator
"Dark mode — less visible at night, when trauma often feels heaviest."
7. Crisis Resources 2:00 - 2:15
Do
Click "Safety Resources" button in sidebar → Modal opens
Narrator
"When high distress is detected, crisis resources are highlighted — 24/7 hotlines available via call or text. We never force them. Users stay in control."
Do
Close the modal
8. Closing 2:15 - 2:30
Show
Return to chat view, URL visible in address bar
Narrator
"SoulShield — encrypted support that hides in plain sight. Available now at soulshield.vercel.app."
End Card
Show logo + URL: soulshield.vercel.app

Total Runtime: ~2:30

Bystander Mode (Scene 4) is the key demo — don't rush it!

Sample Prompts to Use

Normal Support

"I can't stop thinking about what happened"
"I feel overwhelmed and don't know what to do"

⭐ Bystander Mode (Most Important)

"He checks my phone every night. I just need something to help me get through today."
"Someone might be watching. I can't talk freely."
"I'm at my job and I can't leave. My manager is very strict."

Grounding

"Help me calm down"
"I'm having a panic attack"

Recording Checklist

  • Browser at 1080p resolution
  • Clear chat history (New Session)
  • Test login works
  • Test voice input works
  • Quiet environment for voiceover
  • URL visible in address bar
  • Slow, steady mouse movements
  • Pause 2-3 seconds after each action

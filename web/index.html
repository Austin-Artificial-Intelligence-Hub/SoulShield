The Problem

Survivors of human trafficking often can't access traditional mental health support. They may be:

  • Monitored — Someone may be checking their phone or watching their screen
  • Isolated — Cut off from friends, family, and professional help
  • Afraid — Seeking help openly could put them in danger
  • Alone at night — When trauma feels heaviest, no one is available

Existing therapy apps fail these survivors because they use obvious clinical language that could be detected by an abuser.

Our Solution

SoulShield is a covert support companion designed specifically for survivors who can't speak freely. We offer:

  • Bystander-Safe Mode — When you indicate someone might be watching, our responses look like a generic wellness app
  • Trauma-Informed AI — Responses are gentle, non-prescriptive, and respect your autonomy
  • Grounding & Regulation — Breathing exercises and calming techniques when you're overwhelmed
  • 24/7 Availability — Support during the late-night hours when therapists aren't available
  • Zero Data Sharing — Encrypted conversations that auto-delete after 30 days

Who We Help

SoulShield supports:

  • Survivors of human trafficking seeking covert emotional support
  • Domestic violence survivors in monitored environments
  • Anyone who needs a calm presence between therapy sessions
  • People experiencing isolation or emotional distress

What We Are Not

SoulShield is not a replacement for professional care. We do not:

  • Diagnose or treat mental health conditions
  • Provide medical, legal, or escape planning advice
  • Replace human therapists or crisis services
  • Make decisions for you — you stay in control

Our Approach

Every response follows trauma-informed principles: safety, choice, collaboration, trustworthiness, and empowerment. We believe you are the expert on your own life. We offer options, not instructions. We listen, reflect, and stay present with you.

1. Intelligent Safety Detection

When you send a message, our Safety Router analyzes it for:

  • Privacy context — Are you alone or might someone be watching?
  • Distress level — Do you need grounding or gentle support?
  • Risk signals — Should we prioritize safety resources?

2. Bystander-Safe Responses

If you indicate someone might be checking your phone (e.g., "He reads my messages"), SoulShield automatically:

  • Uses neutral, everyday language that won't raise suspicion
  • Avoids words like "abuse," "escape," or "police"
  • Looks like a generic wellness app to anyone watching

3. Trauma-Informed Support

Our Support Coach follows trauma-informed principles:

  • Offers choices, never commands
  • Uses your own words when reflecting
  • Respects your pace and boundaries
  • Guides grounding exercises when you're overwhelmed

4. Voice Support

Can't type? Use voice input to speak your message. Enable voice replies to hear responses read aloud — helpful when reading is difficult.

5. Privacy by Design

Your data is encrypted, auto-deleted after 30 days, and never shared. We can't read your conversations.

What "Encrypted" Means

Imagine putting your message in a locked box that only you have the key to. That's encryption. Even if someone intercepted your message, they couldn't read it — it would look like random letters and numbers.

Password Security

Your password is never stored as plain text. We use a technique called "hashing" that turns your password into a unique code. Even we can't see your actual password.

Safe Messaging

If you're in a situation where someone might check your phone, our responses are designed to look like a normal wellness app. We avoid obvious therapy language when you indicate you might not have privacy.

Your Rights

  • You can log out anytime to clear your session
  • You can start a new session to begin fresh
  • All data automatically deletes after 30 days
  • We never share your data with anyone

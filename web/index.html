About SoulShield

SoulShield is a trauma-informed companion designed to help you feel less alone during difficult moments. We're here between your therapy sessions, late at night, or whenever you need a calm presence.

What We Are

A supportive friend who listens without judgment. We help you:

  • Process your feelings through gentle reflection
  • Practice grounding when you feel overwhelmed
  • Prepare for therapy conversations
  • Feel less alone in hard moments

What We Are Not

SoulShield is not a replacement for professional mental health care. We do not:

  • Diagnose or treat mental health conditions
  • Provide medical or legal advice
  • Replace human connection or therapy
  • Make decisions for you

Our Approach

Every response is designed with trauma-informed principles. We believe you are the expert on your own life. We offer choices, not instructions. We listen, reflect, and stay present with you.

How It Works

Using SoulShield is simple. Just type what's on your mind, and I'll be here to listen and support you.

1. Share What You're Feeling

Type whatever you're comfortable sharing. You can be as brief or detailed as you like. There's no right or wrong way to express yourself here.

2. I Listen and Respond

I'll reflect what I hear, validate your feelings, and offer gentle options — like grounding exercises or questions to help you process.

3. You Stay in Control

You decide what we talk about, how deep we go, and when we stop. I never push or pressure. This is your space.

4. Grounding When You Need It

If you're feeling overwhelmed, I can guide you through simple breathing or sensory exercises to help you feel more present and calm.

5. Your Privacy Is Protected

Everything you share is encrypted and automatically deleted after 30 days. No one reads your conversations — not even us.

Privacy & Security

Your safety and privacy are our highest priorities. Here's how we protect you:

Three Layers of Protection

⬢

Encryption

Your messages are scrambled into unreadable code

◯

◇

Zero Sharing

What "Encrypted" Means

Imagine putting your message in a locked box that only you have the key to. That's encryption. Even if someone intercepted your message, they couldn't read it — it would look like random letters and numbers.

Password Security

Your password is never stored as plain text. We use a technique called "hashing" that turns your password into a unique code. Even we can't see your actual password.

Safe Messaging

If you're in a situation where someone might check your phone, our responses are designed to look like a normal wellness app. We avoid obvious therapy language when you indicate you might not have privacy.

Your Rights

  • You can log out anytime to clear your session
  • You can start a new session to begin fresh
  • All data automatically deletes after 30 days
  • We never share your data with anyone
